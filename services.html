Our Service Keeps you Smiling

Welcome to Dr. Patel's Dental Clinic

Join us on a journey to a healthy and beautiful smile.

Root Canal Treatment


Root canal treatment is a dental procedure aimed at removing infected or damaged pulp from tooth to relieve pain and preserve the tooth structure.

Crowns and Veneers


Dental crowns and veneers are tooth-shaped "caps" placed over damaged teeth to restore their shape, size, strength, and improve appearance.

Dental Implants


Dental implants, made of titanium are surgically placed into the jawbone that provides support for the replacement of missing teeth.

Smile Designing


Smile design involves enhancing the appearance of a person's smile in cases when front teeth are irregulary shaped, discoloured, broken or worn out.

Dental Fillings


Dental cement and composite are used for fillings to restore the tooth structure after removing the carious part from the infected tooth.

Braces and Aligners


They are orthodontic devices used to straighten teeth and correct bite issues by applying gentle pressure to gradually move teeth into their desired positions.

Pediatric Dentistry


Pediatric dentistry is specifically tailored to infants, children and adolescents, addressing their unique dental needs and promoting lifelong oral health habits.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation (FMR)

FMR is designed to restore the function, health and aesthetics of the entire mouth in cases of missing teeth, worn out teeth, damaged teeth or bite problems by using dental implants, crowns or bridges.

Dentures


Dentures are used to replace multiple/all missing teeth, designed to restore chewing function and improve the appearance of the smile.

Teeth cleaning


Teeth cleaning or Scaling involves removal of plaque, tartar and stains from the teeth to maintain good oral health and prevent gum disease.

Teeth whithening


Teeth whitening is aimed at lightening the shade of the teeth by removing stains and discoloration by using bleaching agents, to enhance the appearance of the smile.

Extraction


Extraction is the painless removal of a tooth from its socket, typically performed when a tooth is severely damaged, decayed or when harming adjacent tooth.
