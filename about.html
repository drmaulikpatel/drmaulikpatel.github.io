About Us

Know Your Doctor Our Vision

Dr. Maulik Patel is a highly skilled and experienced dentist specializing in Prosthodontics and Implantology. With a Bachelor's degree in Dental Surgery (B.D.S.) from Karnavati School of Dentistry (KSD) and a Master's degree (M.D.S.) from Ahmedabad Dental College and Hospital (ADCH), Dr. Maulik Patel is dedicated to provide top-notch dental treatments to his patients. Dr. Maulik Patel had also been associated with Gujarat Cancer Research Institute (GCRI) for the oral and maxillofacial prosthetic rehabilitation in cancer patients. He is also extensively trained in Prosthodontics and Implantology, a specialized branch of dentistry focusing on the restoration and replacement of teeth to improve both function and aesthetics. Dr. Patel's expertise in Prosthodontics allows him to offer a wide range of services including Crowns, Bridges, Dental Veneers, Dental Implants, Smile Design & Smile Makeover, Implant supported fixed teeth and dentures, along with General dentistry tailored to each patient's unique needs and goals.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Patel also offers consultations at various clinics across Ahmedabad.

With a passion for staying at the forefront of his field, Dr. Patel regularly attends national and international conferences to ensure he is up-to-date with the latest advancements and techniques in the field of dentistry. Whether you're in need of routine dental care, cosmetic enhancements, or complex restorative treatments, you can trust Dr. Maulik Patel and his dedicated team to deliver exceptional results with a personalized touch.

At Dr. Patel's Dental Clinic, our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in dental care, where patients feel valued, respected, and empowered to achieve optimal oral health and beautiful smiles. We envision a future where every patient who walks through our doors experiences the warmth of our hospitality, the expertise of our clinicians, and the satisfaction of knowing they are receiving the best possible care. We believe that everyone deserves access to exceptional dental care, hence we are dedicated to providing all dental treatments at a reasonable rate and flexible payment options, without compromising on our standards and quality of treatment.
