Root Canal Treatment


Root canal treatment is a dental procedure aimed at removing infected or damaged pulp from tooth to relieve pain and preserve the tooth structure.

Crowns and Veneers


Dental crowns and veneers are tooth-shaped "caps" placed over damaged teeth to restore their shape, size, strength, and improve appearance.

Dental Implants


Dental implants, made of titanium are surgically placed into the jawbone that provides support for the replacement of missing teeth.

Smile Designing


Smile design involves enhancing the appearance of a person's smile in cases when front teeth are irregulary shaped, discoloured, broken or worn out.
